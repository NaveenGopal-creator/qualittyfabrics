About Qualitty Fabrics

A vertically integrated textile fabric manufacturer in Perundurai, Erode, Tamil Nadu — continuously investing and expanding to remain competitive on global textile markets.

Est. 2004

Our Story

Two decades of excellence

Qualitty Fabrics was founded with a clear vision — to bring world-class textile manufacturing capability to South India while maintaining an unwavering commitment to quality and sustainability.

Starting as a small weaving unit in Perundurai, we continuously realized investments and expanded to become a fully vertical fabrics manufacturer. Today our facility is equipped with modern in-house Sulzer Looms and Airjet Looms that power our production of MultiLayer, Dobby, Jacquard, Yarn Dyed, Stripe Satin, Satin and Special Fabrics.

We adopt updated monitoring systems at every stage to ensure our products meet global standards and the specific expectations of each customer — from fast-fashion brands to luxury textile houses worldwide.

Our Facility

State-of-the-art infrastructure

Our Perundurai facility is equipped with the latest weaving and testing technology to produce consistent, premium-grade fabrics at scale.

  • Sulzer Looms — High-speed rapier weaving for precision pattern control
  • Airjet Looms — High-volume production with consistent quality
  • In-house Lab — Full testing: shrinkage, colorfastness, tensile strength
  • Quality Monitoring — Real-time inspection at every production stage
  • Vertical Integration — Yarn to finished fabric under one roof
  • Export Packaging — International packing standards with full documentation
